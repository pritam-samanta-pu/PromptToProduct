Rules

Keep It
Simple

This is a knowledge event, not a competition. Just a few things to keep in mind.

01
Team of Max 3
Form a team of 1 to 3 students. Solo participation is allowed.
02
Pick One Topic
Choose one of the 5 given topics on Day 1 and build your app around it.
03
Use the Stack
Your app must be built using Flutter with Firebase Auth & Firestore as the backend.
04
No Rewards
This event is purely for learning. No prizes, no certificates — just knowledge and experience.
05
Document Everything
Maintain project documentation alongside your build. Submit it before the Showcase Day.
Deliverables

What to
Submit

By Showcase Day (Day 2), each team submits and presents three things.

📱
Working Flutter App
A functional Flutter mobile app with Firebase Auth & Firestore built around your chosen topic.
📄
Project Documentation
Cover your problem statement, tech decisions, app architecture, agent flow, screenshots, and what you learned.
🎤
Live Demo
A short walkthrough of your app in front of the group on Showcase Day (Day 2). Show what you built and how it works.
